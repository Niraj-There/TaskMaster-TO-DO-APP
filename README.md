# ✅ TaskMaster - Your Easy Todo App

TaskMaster is a modern and simple app to help you organize your daily tasks. It looks good and has all the tools you need to stay productive!

## ✨ What it Does (Features)

* **Add Tasks:** Quickly add new tasks with a title, notes, and a due date.
* **Edit Tasks:** Easily change your tasks.
* **Mark Done:** Check off tasks when you finish them.
* **Delete Tasks:** Remove tasks you don't need.
* **Clear All Done:** Delete all finished tasks at once.
* **Filter Tasks:** See only "All," "Incomplete," or "Completed" tasks.
* **Sort Tasks:** Arrange tasks by:
    * Newest (default)
    * Oldest
    * Title (A-Z)
    * Due Date
* **Search Tasks:** Find tasks by typing in their title or notes.
* **Due Date Alerts:** See if tasks are overdue (red) or due very soon (yellow).
* **Task Stats:** See how many tasks you have in total, how many are left, and how many are done.
* **Saves Your Tasks:** Your tasks are saved in your browser, so they're still there even if you close the app.
* **Dark Mode:** Switch between a light and dark look. The app remembers your choice!
* **Works Everywhere:** Looks good on phones, tablets, and computers.
* **Simple Messages:** Get small pop-up messages when you add, edit, or finish tasks.
* **Quick Keys:** Use keyboard shortcuts to add tasks (`Ctrl/Cmd + Enter`) or clear search (`Escape`).

## 🚀 How to Use
